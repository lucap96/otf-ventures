Section 02 — Why We Win
Why We Win
Access is the alpha. We built the engine to capture it.

The best founders do not want another investor. They want someone who has made it – who has scaled from zero, made the first uncomfortable hire, pushed a product into a new market with no playbook. Not advice. Experience. That distinction is everything.

01
The few who have actually done it

There are thousands of people in Europe who call themselves operators. Very few went from zero to scale, raised hundreds of millions, made thousands of hires, navigated every crisis a company can face, and have the scar tissue to prove it.

Our {Ωperators} sit in that group. $1.2B+ raised collectively, 3,000+ hires, two public listings. When a founder hits a real inflection point, someone in this group has lived that exact moment – not in theory, but under pressure.

02
Everyone else competes on the same things

Brand. Check size. The promise of a network. In a market with €59B in European VC dry powder – up 354% since 2011 – those are commodities. The best founders in the best rounds no longer optimize for capital. They choose who they want in the room.

Access has become the constraint. OTF exists to solve it.

03
Other VCs bring us in because we play alongside them

OTF writes small, uncomplicated checks. We do not take whole rounds or compete for board seats. Lead funds lead – but managing operator presence on a cap table is a different job. That gap exists in every institutional round. We fill it.

Roughly half our deals come directly from VCs who bring us in because they want our {Ωperators} alongside them. We are not competing for a slot. We are filling a need no one else is solving at scale. In 2025 we saw ~1,500 deals. The best ones, we were invited into.
