The Model
Operator proximity where it's decisive.
Institutional rigour where it's required.

OTF is built as a high-trust partnership between institutional GPs and elite operators — each bringing a distinct edge to the same objective.

Operators extend our reach into founder networks, pressure-test opportunities through lived experience, and win allocations through founder-to-founder trust. GPs provide the sourcing infrastructure, co-investor relationships, and fund-level discipline. The two functions are deliberately designed to complement rather than overlap: operator proximity where it's decisive, institutional rigour where it's required.

Every investment reflects both lenses. The result is a model that feels operator-led at the company level, while remaining accountable to the standards of a professionally managed venture fund.
