"Show me the incentive and I will show you the outcome."

OTF is not a scout fund. The distinction is structural and deliberate. We thoughtfully put in place incentives to drive desired behaviour.

Scout models cost nothing. No capital at risk, pure upside, paid at the deal level. That sounds attractive, but it optimises for the wrong thing. Scouts disengage after the cheque is written because their outcome is already locked in.

We built the opposite. {Ωperators} commit real capital into the fund on the same terms as every LP. On top of that, 10% of the GP carry pool is reserved exclusively for {Ωperators}, distributed at the end of the fund life, based on each {Ωperator}'s actual contribution to returns. No one gets paid for making the intro. Everyone gets paid when the fund wins.

Whilst the economic incentives are an aspect of why {Ωperators} work with us, the real incentives go beyond this as we essentially provide access to the best founders and scale by taking care of the entire admin. In other words, the platform of a fund without the burden of admin, legal, finance etc. Here is the original document that we shared with them.
01 · Skin in the game
01
Every {Ωperator} commits capital. Every {Ωperator} has something to lose.

Each {Ωperator} enters the fund as an LP, committing real capital alongside every other investor. Their financial outcome is inseparable from fund performance. There is no free ride, no guaranteed payout, no exit before the work is done.

This is the foundation of collective alignment. When the fund wins, every {Ωperator} wins. When it does not, they feel it too. That shared exposure is what keeps the group honest, engaged, and pulling in the same direction over the full life of the fund.

02 · Individual upside
02
{Ωperators} earn carry. Exactly like a GP.

A traditional GP makes money in two ways: their LP investment appreciates with the fund, and they earn carry on the returns they generate. OTF {Ωperators} have the same structure. They are LPs in the fund, and they have a dedicated carry pool on top of that – 10% of total GP carry, reserved exclusively for them.

It is held until the end of the fund life and distributed in proportion to each {Ωperator}'s actual contribution to returns. Not activity, not effort, not presence. Outcomes. Every deal has a primary {Ωperator} assigned at the point of investment. At fund close, once total DPI is known, each {Ωperator} receives a share of the pool proportional to how much their deals drove the overall result. An {Ωperator} whose deals drove 30% of fund returns receives 30% of the pool – equivalent to 3% of total GP carry.

03 · The result
03
A structure that rewards the group and credits the individual.

The collective layer ensures every {Ωperator} is invested in the fund's success, not just their own deals. The individual layer ensures that those who source the winners, support them through the hard moments, and drive the returns that matter are compensated accordingly.

{Ωperators} who show up, back the right founders, and do the work get paid like the GPs they are. Built on three years of trust. Designed to keep it.
